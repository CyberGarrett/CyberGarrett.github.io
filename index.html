About Me

Hello!

My name is Seth, I love puzzles, learning new things, the outdoors, and most things nerd-adjacent. With a unique combination of technical expertise and business acumen, my journey has ranged from co-founding a coffee shop as an undergraduate to conducting cutting-edge cybersecurity research. I started my academic journey with a degree in electrical engineering with a math minor, and ended up with a masters and PhD in computer science.

After turning to computer science for graduate school, my research focused on multifactor authentication (MFA), log analytics, and human factors in cybersecurity, with hands-on experience in machine learning, data pipelines, and security operations. My broad interests and passion bridge the gap between technical implementation and business strategy, having worked everywhere from NASA to a boutique marketing firm. Similarly, I'm comfortable both leading and contributing in a multi-disciplinary team environment.

I have a rich entrepreneurial background, having co-founded a coffee shop and competed in multiple pitch competitions, most recently winning a cash prize in the Hurricane Pitch Competition before graduating. My most recent work experience is developing agentic AI solutions through my consultancy, Deft LLC.

Research and Publications

Expanding Authentication Log Utility ... Dissertation

PhD dissertation that develops a user-centric log processing framework to empirically measure the authentication experience. Authentication events are characterized by time cost, friction from errors, error attribution, and resultant performance behaviors. Analysis of economic, psychological, and security insights follows.
